My PhD Thesis, in Haiku

On the day I defended my PhD thesis, I sat in a bar waiting for my friends to come celebrate, reflecting on my recent experiences. In a rare mood of non-verbose contemplation, I rendered my thesis in Haiku. It is an excersize I now recommend to all recent Doctoral graduates.

 

Modeling Solvent Effects in Optical Lithography

When heat is applied
Solvent flows through polymer.
Your model is wrong.
