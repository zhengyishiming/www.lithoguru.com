Gentleman

   1. A man of gentle or noble birth or superior social position.
   2. A well-mannered and considerate man with high standards of proper behavior.
   3. A man of independent means who does not need to have a wage-paying job.
   4. A man.

I can say with some certainty that definition #4 applies to me. But I also have a goal of achieving 2&3 (#1 holds no appeal for me). In any case, these pages show some of my interests that have little to no chance of producing income. I'll let the gentle reader decide if they exhibit "high standards of proper behavior".
